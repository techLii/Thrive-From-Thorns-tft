# Who We Are

Thrive from Thorns is a registered non-profit company limited by guarantee under the Kenya Companies Act, 2015. We exist to restore dignity and strength to those facing the pressing challenges of poverty, broken families, and youth unemployment. 

Through a holistic approach—incorporating education, mentorship, healthcare, and economic empowerment—we equip individuals to overcome adversity and realize their full potential. We are a non-political, inclusive, and ethically governed organization, ensuring that every resource is dedicated solely to our mission.

# Our Model: The HEDEC Approach

We follow a comprehensive four-pillar approach designed to transform lives from the inside out:

1.  **HEAL – Trauma Healing & Wellbeing**
    *   *Focus:* Emotional, physical, and spiritual restoration.
    *   *Approach:* Counseling, mentorship, and community health programs to strengthen resilience.

2.  **DISCOVER – Purpose & Identity**
    *   *Focus:* Uncovering life purpose, values, and talents.
    *   *Approach:* Workshops and labs that empower individuals to understand their potential.

3.  **EMPOWER – Education & Economic Skills**
    *   *Focus:* Knowledge, skill development, and self-sufficiency.
    *   *Approach:* Academic support, vocational training, and innovation hubs.

4.  **CONTRIBUTE – Leadership & Community Impact**
    *   *Focus:* Service, leadership, and giving back.
    *   *Approach:* Peer mentorship and community transformation projects.
